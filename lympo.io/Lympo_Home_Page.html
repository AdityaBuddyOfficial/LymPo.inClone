All LMT owners can now claim the $SPORT tokens they are entitled to. Choose your claiming option, based on the time you acquired LMT tokens or press “Check Calculations” to see data from both Snapshots. More info can be found in our blog
Snapshot 1 $SPORT claim

Everyone who acquired LMT tokens BEFORE January 10, 2022, 06:24:19 AM UTC can claim the SPORT tokens with a 1:1 ratio (SPORT to LMT). All tokens of this phase are for immediate claim.

Snapshot 2 $SPORT claim

We are distributing $SPORT tokens to everyone who bought LMT tokens between January 10th and January 16th, 2022. The tokens are vested, they will be unlocked over a period of 3 years and they can be claimed at anytime.
